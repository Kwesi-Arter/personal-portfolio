About Me

I am a passionate graphic designer with more than 10 years of experience in the field. I specialize in creating designs that are visually appealing, functional and user-friendly.

My journey as a graphic designer started about 12 years ago, when I decided to pursue my passion for art and design. I earned a Visual Art Certification from St. Margaret Mary Senior High School (Ghana) and other graphic design certification programs. Since then, I have worked with a wide range of clients across various industries.

As a graphic designer, my goal is to create designs that not only look great, but also effectively communicate the client's message to their target audience. I strive to understand the client's needs, vision and brand identity, and then use my creativity and design skills to bring their ideas to life.

More About Me || Skills

My expertise includes creating designs for branding, marketing, advertising, print and digital media. I am proficient in using a range of design tools and software, including Adobe Creative Suite, Affinity Creative Apps, Sketch, Figma and more.

I am proud to say that my work has been recognized by several leading small and big scale industries and publications. However, the most rewarding part of my job is seeing my designs make a real impact on my client's business.

When I am not designing, you can find me exploring new creative ideas, emerging design tools or applications, places, and spending time with my family and friends.

I appreciate you for checking out my portfolio and I look forward to working with you someday soon!
